LEARN

Offering dozens of informational and interactive workshops, Fall Training Conference provides limitless opportunities to gain knowledge in a wide array of topics like leadership, professionalism, and, of course, all things Circle K! Whether you're hosting a workshop to share your expertise or attending one for the first time, FTC will give you the opportunity to share and experience new perspectives and ideas.

NETWORK

As one of our district's premier events, Fall Training Conference provides countless opportunities to meet fellow Circle K members from all over California, Nevada, and Hawaii! With several hundred attendees and a rich, established schedule of activities, FTC is sure to connect you with others just like you. Make new friends, establish new bonds, and expand your Circle K network!

WELCOME

Each year, Fall Training Conference hosts hundreds of new members from clubs and divisions all over CNH. We make it a priority to provide a warm welcome for these members with a new member induction and several activities that allow everyone to gain a better sense of what Circle K International is and what we stand for.

ENJOY

Above all, Fall Training Conference is a time for you to enjoy yourself and create long-lasting memories with your CKI friends! With close team activities, amazing campfire skits, and spirited general sessions, FTC really is a Circle K experience for all to treasure.
